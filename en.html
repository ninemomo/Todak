Privacy Policy

Todak Time
Privacy Policy

Effective April 28, 2025  ·  Last updated April 28, 2025

Core Principle

Todak does not operate any developer servers.
All data is stored solely on your device or in your personal iCloud account.
The developer has no access to your data.

01

Overview

Todak Time is a subject-based focus timer app for anyone studying for exams.

This policy explains what information is processed and how when you use the app.

02

Information We Store

The following data is stored on your device:

ItemContentStorage
SubjectsSubject name, color, daily goalDevice / iCloud (optional)
Study sessionsStart/end time, subject name, durationDevice / iCloud (optional)
Exam schedulesExam name, dateDevice / iCloud (optional)
App settingsLanguage, appearance, haptics, notification intervalDevice only

All data is entered directly by the user. The app does not collect data automatically.

03

Information We Do Not Collect

Todak does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data (GPS or IP-based)
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, etc.)
  • Device unique identifiers
  • Browsing or search history
  • Data from other apps
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Behavioral analytics (no analytics SDK)
  • Social login information
04

iCloud Sync

If iCloud sync is enabled, your subjects, sessions, and schedules are synced across devices via your personal iCloud account.

  • The developer cannot access synced data.
  • Apple's CloudKit infrastructure is used, subject to the Apple Privacy Policy.
  • iCloud sync can be disabled at any time in app settings.
  • When disabled, data remains only on the local device.
05

In-App Purchases

Premium feature purchases are processed exclusively through the Apple App Store.

  • Payment information is never shared with the developer.
  • Purchase verification occurs only between Apple's servers and your device.
  • Refunds are handled under Apple's refund policy.
06

Local Notifications

The app sends the following notifications when configured by the user:

  • Timer check-in reminders (at your chosen interval)
  • Exam D-day countdown notifications

All notifications are generated on-device. No external server is involved. Notification permission can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings.

07

Third-Party Sharing

Todak does not sell, rent, or share user data with any third party. Apple services used by the app:

ServicePurpose
CloudKitiCloud sync (only when enabled by user)
App Store / StoreKitIn-app purchase processing
UNUserNotificationsLocal notification delivery (no server)

All services are subject to the Apple Privacy Policy.

08

Data Retention & Deletion

SituationResult
App deletionAll on-device data deleted immediately
iCloud data removalSettings → [Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Todak → Delete
In-app resetSettings → "Reset All Data"

No data is retained on developer servers, so no deletion request to the developer is necessary.

09

Children's Privacy

Todak does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. Since no personal identifiers are collected, the same level of protection applies to all users regardless of age.

11

Security

  • On-device data is protected by iOS sandbox security.
  • iCloud sync data is encrypted by Apple in transit and at rest.
  • No developer server means no risk of server-side data breach.
12

Policy Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be updated. Significant changes will be noted in the app update release notes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
